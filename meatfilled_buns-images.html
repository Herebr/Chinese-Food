Meatfilled BunsXiaolongbao, also known as Meatfilled Buns, is a famous Chinese delicacy and a famous Chinese snack. It is a famous traditional snack in Changzhou, Wuxi, Suzhou, Shanghai, Nanjing, Jiaxing and other regions south of the Yangtze River. Xiaolongbao originated from the soup filling dumplings in Kaifeng, the capital city of the Northern Song Dynasty. It was inherited, developed and evolved in the south of the Yangtze River in the Southern Song Dynasty. The common characteristics of stewed feet, delicious and delicious.
